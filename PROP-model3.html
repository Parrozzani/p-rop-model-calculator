Retinopathy of Prematurity
Risk Assessment

A validated clinical decision support tool for predicting Type 1 ROP in preterm infants using the P-ROP logistic regression model.

Validated Model

Based exclusively on parameters readily available immediately after birth (Gestational Age, Birth Weight, Platelet counts).

Immediate Stratification

Instantly classifies infants as Low or High Risk using a validated probability cutoff (0.449) early after birth.

Clinical Support

Provides clinical support in early referral of high-risk infants to tertiary care centers preserving the disease’s narrow therapeutic window for effective treatment.

Privacy & Terms of Use

1. Data Privacy & Local Computation

The P-ROP calculator was developed as a standalone clinician-side tool, in which all computations are performed locally on the user’s device, similarly to a standard calculator.

  • No clinical data are transmitted, stored, or logged.
  • No patient data are stored on any server or cloud database.
  • This ensures full compliance with data privacy and confidentiality standards.

2. Clinical Disclaimer

This application is intended for research and clinical support purposes only.
